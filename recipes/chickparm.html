Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is no doubt the most popular non-pasta dish in Italian-American style restaurants. And while this delicious meal may seem fancy, it's actually pretty easy to make at home.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • All purpose flour
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Provolone Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450
  2. Pound chicken
  3. Season chicken
  4. Dip chicken in flour and eggs twice
  5. Fry chicken in olive oil until golden brown
  6. Transfer chicken to baking dish add sauce and provolone cheese
  7. Bake in oven in cheese in browned
  8. Enjoy
